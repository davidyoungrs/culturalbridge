### 1.2 Ethical Data Sourcing and Integrity
Consistent with CMI’s focus on **Ethical Decision-Making**, the platform prioritizes data integrity. We utilize "Sovereign-Grade" intelligence from the **World Health Organization (WHO)**, **TuGo**, and the **UK Foreign, Commonwealth & Development Office (FCDO)**. By providing explicit attributions and adhering to FAIR (Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, Reusable) principles, the project ensures that managers are making decisions based on verified, high-fidelity information rather than hearsay or social-media sentiment.
